Upcoming Video — A Shift in Perspective

Caroline Madeleine JULIEN

Video announcement

I am currently preparing a short video on the shift in perspective required between a bank and its ICT service provider, using the metaphor of the Starman watching from above.

The video will explore why imagining what the other party sees is not intuitive, and why this shift in perspective is essential for resilience, compliance, and operational risk management.

Planned publication date: 12 June 2026

Topic

The video addresses the relationship between financial entities and ICT service providers in the context of digital operational resilience.

It focuses on the need to open the “black box” between the bank and the ICT provider, and to create a clearer shared understanding of responsibilities, risks, dependencies, and blind spots.

It will also introduce how the EU Cyber Resilience Act and the SBOM can support better transparency, collaboration, and control across the digital supply chain.

Related event

These topics will be explored further during the SKOLAE Training Day in Luxembourg.
